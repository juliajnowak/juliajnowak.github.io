How is looking at something like the Earth from above a fantasy?

Looking at the Earth from above is not something anyone can really just wake up, go outside, and do.
It's not some readily accessible thing. At the minimum, we might experience that while in a window seat on an airplane, some not even getting a chance to even do that. As astonishing as a window plane can be, it's nothing compared to what I imagine astronauts feel when they reach space and see the Earth from their ship. The idea of looking down on Earth from somewhere like space is a fantasy to ordinary people, it's not something we will probably ever have the experience of doing, minus the very small amount of those who make it as an astronaut.
